FAQ's

  • How can I cancel my booking?

    • To cancel a booking, simply visit the manage bookings tab whilst signed into your account and follow the on-screen instructions.

  • How far in advanced can I book my hotel?

    • You can book a hotel up to three months in advance. It is also worth noting discounts of up to 20% will be received should you book between 80-90 days in advanced, this discount will slowly diminish the closer to the booking date you decide to book.

  • Are there any charges incurred for cancelling a booking?

    • As long as you cancel 60 days before the booking date, there will be no cancellation fees. If, however, you cancel between 30 and 60 days of the booking date, there will be charges of up to 50% of the booking price. Within 30 days of the booking date, 100% of the booking price will be charged.

  • Will I recieve a receipt for my order?

    • You will receive a digital receipt after a payment has been made.

  • Do you accept payment in different currencies?

    • We currently accept payments in GBP, Euros and USD. Our exchange rates are as follows:
    1 GBP = 1.2 Euros
    1 GBP = 1.6 USD.

  • Other than the contact form, are there other methods to contact your company?
